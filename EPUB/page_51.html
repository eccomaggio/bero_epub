Melted Method This is a quick, easy-to-follow method, sometimes used for biscuits, buns and syrup cakes, such as Gingerbread; these cakes are sometimes rather hard on the outside when fresh, but improve to become soft and slightly sticky if kept for two days or more before cutting. Be-Ro Brownies 2 oz Plain Chocolate 3 oz Margarine 2 Eggs 5 oz Caster Sugar 4 oz Be-Ro S.R. Flour 5 teaspoon Salt + cup Chopped Nuts 1 Melt together chocolate and margarine over hot water 2 Beat in eggs and sugar 3 Mix in flour, salt and nuts. Spread in well greased 8 inch square tin 4 Bake in moderate oven be left when the top is (350°F, gas mark 4) for 30-35 touched lightly with a finger minutes until top has dull 5 Cool slightly, then cut into crust.
