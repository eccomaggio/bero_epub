Rubbed-in Fruit Cake Basic Recipe 8 oz Be-Ro S.R. Flour + teaspoon Salt 4 oz Margarine 4 oz Caster Sugar 2 Eggs 2 tablespoons Milk 6 oz Mixed Dried Fruit or other variation — see 1 Place flour and salt in bowl, rub in margarine 2 Add sugar and fruit. Beat together eggs and milk 3 Mix all together to make a soft dropping consistency 4 Place in greased 6 inch or 7 inch cake tin below 5 Bake in moderate oven (350°F, gas mark 4) for 1-14 hours Variations Lemon and Sultana — use 6 oz sultanas and add grated rind of a lemon Pineapple — 4 oz glacé pineapple. Finely chop 3 oz and add to cake, slice remaining ounce and place on the top Orange and Spice — add 1 teaspoon mixed spice and coarsely grated rind of an orange to the basic mixture Fruit and Nut — add 4 oz raisins and 2 oz chopped almonds or walnuts Date — 3 oz chopped dates added with sugar Chocolate Chip — add 3-4 oz chocolate, finely chopped Milk Chocolate Cake 7 oz Be-Ro S.R. Flour 8 oz Caster Sugar 2 teaspoon Salt 2 tablespoons Cocoa 4 oz Margarine 2 Eggs beaten with 5 tablespoons Evaporated Milk 5 tablespoons Water 1 teaspoon Vanilla Essence 1 Sift together flour, sugar, salt and cocoa 2 Rub in margarine 5 Bake in moderate oven 3 Stir in eggs, essence and liquids; beat well 4 Grease and flour 2 deep 7 inch cake tins, not loose-bottomed as the mixture would run out (325-350°F, gas mark 3-4) about 35 minutes 6 When cold, sandwich together and cover with Milk Chocolate Icing (page 78) Page 39 , |
